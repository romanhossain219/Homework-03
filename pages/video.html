Video tag has so many attributes. Such as (Autoplay, controls, loop, muted, preload, src, poster, width, height)


width: It is better not to use height in video tag. Height takes automatically when we give width to the video.

autoplay: Chromium browsers do not allow autoplay in most cases. However, muted autoplay is always allowed. Add muted after autoplay to let your video file start playing automatically (but muted).
